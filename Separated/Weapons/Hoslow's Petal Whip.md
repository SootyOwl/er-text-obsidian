### Hoslow's Petal Whip [20050000]

Metal whip formed of razor-sharp chain-link blades that have the appearance of flower petals. This work of art is handed down through the generations of the illustrious House Hoslow. High dexterity is required to wield this weapon, but it excels at inflicting devastating blood loss. "The tale of House Hoslow is told in blood."### Hoslow's Petal Whip [20050000]

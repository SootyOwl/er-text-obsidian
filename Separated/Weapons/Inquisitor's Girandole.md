### Inquisitor's Girandole [16130000]

Instrument of torture used on nobles behind the curtain at the Volcano Manor of Mt. Gelmir.

Its numerous spikes pierce the flesh, then singe the wounds with flame. The smell of burnt blood induces despair in the victim. A candlestick conceived by a thorough mind.### Inquisitor's Girandole [16130000]

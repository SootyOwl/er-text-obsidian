### Visage Shield [32160000]

Tricksome bronze shield depicting the face of a Fire Giant. Several tongues leap from its open mouth.

The dreadful visage and burning flames are designed to remind one of the horror of facing a Fire Giant. In other words, this shield has an instructional function.### Visage Shield [32160000]
